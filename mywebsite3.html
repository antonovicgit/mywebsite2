Course Overview

This course is designed to teach you the art and business of event photography. From understanding your equipment to delivering the final edited photos, you will learn the skills needed to succeed as an event photographer.

Course Objectives

  • Understand the basics of event photography and necessary equipment.
  • Learn how to prepare for various types of events.
  • Master the techniques for capturing high-quality images in different lighting conditions.
  • Develop skills in post-processing and editing photos.
  • Gain insights into marketing and managing an event photography business.
